Motivation

I started working in the film industry during my MA studies in January 2014. In the following 10+ I took part in several productions of different size as a 2nd Assistant Director.

However, the instability and sinking number of jobs, especially in Hungary has made me turn towards another field, on that can provide a safe and reliable employment.
I started learning Python in February 2024 through a course on Udemy and found the process and the knowledge acquired interesting, challenging and all-round pleasant.
After this course I plan to take a look into other aspects of coding, other languages and applications, and in the meantime look for junior positions to start my new career.
